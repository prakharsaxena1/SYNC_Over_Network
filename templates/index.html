HOW TO USE?

Start the server and go to the server's IP address to connect the devices. Do not paste before connecting the devices; else, the pastes will not appear on other devices.

To copy the paste's content, simply click on it, and it will be transferred to your device's clipboard.

To retrieve the server's IP address, go to the connected devices tab and click on the server's name.
