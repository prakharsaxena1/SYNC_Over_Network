HOW TO USE?

This is a quick tutorial on how to use the SYNC - Over - Network app.
Open the webpage in the devices with which you wish to share your clipboard after running the server. Now, on the sending device, paste text into the input one by one, and then hit sync on the receiving device.
The "SYNC" button just syncs the clipboard with the most recent sender.
Simply click the "clean" button to remove all of the pastes.
To copy the message from the pastes, click on the pastes and the text will be copied to your device.
To remove a single paste from the clipboard and press sync on the receiving device.
